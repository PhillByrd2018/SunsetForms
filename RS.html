A native of Valley, AL, Dr. Dollar was a graduate of Valley High School, where he was president of the student council. He received his B.S. from Samford University in 1965, where he was a member of Sigma Nu Fraternity and ODK Honorary. Upon graduation, he attended the University of Alabama School of Dentistry at Birmingham, where he served as president of both the freshman and sophomore classes. Dr. Dollar served in the U.S. Navy, including a year with the First Marine Aircraft Wing in Vietnam. In 1971, he opened his dental practice in Dothan. His 44-year professional career included serving as president of the Alabama Academy of General Dentistry and president of the University of Alabama School of Dentistry Alumni Association.
